How It Works

1. Set "Override Mode" to ON and choose a custom time

2. Click "Save Settings" - Raspberry Pi will read this setting

3. When you scan a card, the scanner will use your custom time

4. The attendance record will show your selected time

💡 Tip: Use this to demonstrate Present (7:30 AM) and Late (8:00 AM) scans anytime!

📋 Time Rules:
• Before 7:45 AM → Present
• 7:45 AM - 8:15 AM → Late
• After 8:15 AM → Invalid (Ignored)
